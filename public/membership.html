Graduate Membership Essential

All graduates of the college are eligible to apply for the graduate membership and all privileges of the membership will be available to members, including the eligibility to cast one vote at all meetings of the alumni association, and to be a member of any committee of the Alumni Association.

All graduates of the college will be unconditionally regarded as a graduate member upon the payment of the membership fee.
