Preface

Dear Members, UGAers and friends,

Welcome to the new website of the HKUGAC Alumni Association (HKUGAC AA)! This site has been designed to better serve the growing community of HKUGAC & HKUGAPS graduates and foster stronger connections among our alumni.

The HKUGAC AA was established in 2012 with the mission of supporting our alma maters and providing opportunities for our members to stay engaged with HKUGAC, HKUGAPS, and one another. Over the past 12 years, we have grown to over 500 active members living around the world.

Whether you graduated last year or years ago, this website is your gateway to the HKUGAC AA community. Here you will find information about our latest initiatives, upcoming events, and ways to get involved.

Our alumni community is a valuable and vibrant network. We hope this new website will help you rediscover the spirit of HKUGAC & HKUGAPS, renew old friendships, and unlock new possibilities. We look forward to welcoming you and working together to advance the legacy of our beloved alma maters.

Sincerely,

The HKUGAC Alumni Association Executive Committee
