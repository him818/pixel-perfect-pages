Privacy Policy

HKUGA College Alumni Association ("HKUGACAA", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the personal data of our graduates, members, and friends. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, store, utilize, and protect your information when you register as a member, use the Alumni Tutoring Network, or join our community events.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering for membership, submitting registration forms, or opting into our tutoring directories. This includes:

  • Full English and Chinese names
  • Graduation year and class section
  • Contact details including email addresses and phone numbers
  • LinkedIn profiles and occupational / university academic records
  • Tutoring preferences and tutoring subject specializations

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected personal information is used exclusively to facilitate and improve the services offered by HKUGACAA, specifically:

  • To manage your alumni membership registry and issue official digital alumni passes
  • To operate the HKUGAC Tutoring Network, helping connect current students with eligible tutors
  • To distribute newsletters, general meeting notices, and updates about alumni networking events
  • To verify graduation status with HKUGA College to maintain community integrity

3. Data Security & Protection

We implement robust security measures to safeguard your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. All information is processed securely, and access to personal records is strictly confined to authorized members of the HKUGACAA Executive Committee.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to access or modify your personal records, or request removal from our directories, please feel free to contact us.
